Services

We provide all the services needed to keep your property maintained to a high standard.

Exterior
House Wash

We offer high-pressure water blasting for the stubborn moss and softer washing options for more delicate parts of your building.

Roof & Gutter
Cleaning

Keep your roof and gutters clean and tidy. While it’s hard for residents to reach these high places, we’re fully licensed for rooftop work.

Professional
Equipment Hire

We offer a small range of cleaning equipment for hire to help you get the job done. From high-intensity vacuum cleaners to scissor lifts.

Gardening /
Landscaping

We can keep one of your property’s greatest assets looking great. Whether you’re wanting to build a new garden or keep the current one maintained.

Driveway Water
Blasting

Don’t let a mossy driveway bring down the beauty of your home. We offer a water blasting service to wash your drive while using a chemical to keep it clean.

Commercial Window
Cleaning

Experienced commercial window cleaners to help your building look pristine, with a licence to use industrial machinery to get the job done at a high standard.
